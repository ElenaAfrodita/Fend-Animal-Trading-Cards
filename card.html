Chinchilla

Chinchillas are nocturnal creatures who are mostly awake at night.

  • Scientific name: Chinchilla brevicaudata
  • Order: Rodentia
  • Family: Chinchillidae
  • Kingdom:: Animalia

Chinchilla has a shorter tail, a thicker neck and shoulders, and shorter ears than C. lanigera. The former species is currently facing extinction; the latter, though rare, can be found in the wild
